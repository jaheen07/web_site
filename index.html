Welcome to the AI Expert Career

Our aims to promote the adoption and awareness of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in Bangladesh. We believe that AI has the potential to revolutionize various industries in Bangladesh, However, there is currently a lack of awareness and knowledge of AI among the general public and businesses in Bangladesh. Our mission is to bridge this gap by providing education, training, and consultancy services related to AI.
