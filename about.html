About Us

Who we are?

Our startup, AI Expert Career (AIEC), aims to promote the adoption and awareness of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in Bangladesh. We believe that AI has the potential to revolutionize various industries in Bangladesh, including healthcare, education, agriculture, and more. However, there is currently a lack of awareness and knowledge of AI among the general public and businesses in Bangladesh. Our mission is to bridge this gap by providing education, training, and consultancy services related to AI. By doing so, we hope to contribute to the growth of the AI industry in Bangladesh and help the country keep pace with the rapidly evolving global AI landscape.
